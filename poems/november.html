november, 2021.

i

fourth month, and you were in the beach.
a bit unbearable starting it without you:
but with no signal, it was out of your control.
and then at some point, you sent me your pics!
i couldn't believe it:
i had the most beautiful girlfriend in the world.

ii

fourth month, and we were excited.
my heart was pumping, my hands were shaking.
there were so many doubts in my mind:
how would i introduce myself?
what would you think of how i look like?
do i shake hands? what do i say?

looking back every now and then, waiting for you to come
i have to admit, my nerves were at their peak
red cheeks, eyes fatigued, knees weak.
and in a moment, i looked back, almost meek

it was you. yellow top, white jacket, jeans.
at this time of writing, i didn't even look at the picture.
i remember it clearly. vivid in my mind.
there was no second thought, i just hugged you
a sense of relief as you hugged me back.
if i could replay a memory forever,
that is what i would choose.
