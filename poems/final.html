to you, my beloved

a year

you are my safe space
you remind me of a field of flowers
you remind me of the beautiful blue ocean
you remind me of a long, cozy road trip at night
you remind me how nice it is
to love, and be loved.

our relationship is an eventful one,
and looking back, it's funny to trace the circumstances
on how we met. such an unlikely series of events
led me to you.

i was sick in june, bored out of my mind.
i never bothered to meet new people, and thought i could wing it
once classes started. i don't know why, but pressing that link
joining calls, meeting new people, meeting you

it was the most wonderful, unplanned event of my life.

in a small amount of time, i discovered so many things
thanks to our relationship, thanks to you!
it was a year of excitement, of new experiences
a year of yearning, to meet my beloved
a year of fun, of many games together
a year of learning, to understand you better

and most of all, a year of love
a love very sweet,
a love grounded in trust,
a love physically far,
a love energizing,
a love giving strength

thank you, des
for a year of us.

love, Justin
