december, 2021.

fifth month, and it was your birthday.

i remember your reaction to the bracelet,
as you unboxed it in front of me, eyes excited
felt very flustered as you wore it every single day
and even now you continue to wear both!

i remember your christmas gift, a drawing of luna.
i remember your cookies,
as my cousins grew to discover who it came from.
they were the most delicious, and even my mom loved them.

i remember ending the year with you, and i realized that it was
the start of what would be the best years of my life.
