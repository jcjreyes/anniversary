january, 2022.

i

sixth month, and you would get a new cat.
at first, your mom didn't want her...
treated her almost like litter
but overtime, it did get better
and i just saw how good at loving you were.

ii

sixth month, and it was my birthday.
it came early, i remembered.
"wait, don't open it yet," you ordered.
i would see for myself later,

that what you got for me was to be my most prized possession.

a cassette tape... and a player
my heart was racing. my eyes were blurring.
"you make my world bubble up"
and my emotions... built up.

the most wonderful gift that i've ever gotten
in all my years of living
there is nothing to compare, because you had set the bar.
it included a poem, and it remains close to my heart.

because as you said:
"for an eternity or as long as i'm here,"
"your love will be safe with me."
with you, i am safe.
